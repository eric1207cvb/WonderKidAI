### Privacy Policy

**WonderKidAI** ("we", "us", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This App is designed specifically for children, and we strictly adhere to the **Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)** and applicable data protection laws.

#### 1. Data Collection & Privacy

We adopt a **"No Personal Data"** policy:

* **No PII Collected:** We do not collect, store, or share any Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as names, physical addresses, phone numbers, or email addresses from children.
* **Local Storage:** All chat history, interaction logs, and "Growth Journey" records are stored **locally on your device**. We do not have access to this data. Deleting the App will permanently remove this data.

#### 2. Usage of AI Features (Voice & Images)

To provide interactive educational features, the App accesses the device's microphone and camera/photo library only with your explicit permission.

* **Voice & Image Data:** User input (voice recordings or food photos) is transmitted securely via encryption to our AI processor (OpenAI API) solely for the purpose of generating real-time responses (e.g., answering questions or estimating calories).
* **Transient Processing:** This data is used strictly for immediate processing and is **not** permanently stored on our servers or used for model training by us.

#### 3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to support the App's functionality:

* **OpenAI:** Used for natural language processing and image analysis.
* **Apple Services:** Used for speech recognition (Siri/Dictation) and text-to-speech.
